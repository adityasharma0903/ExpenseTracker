About the Event

Join us for TechFest 2023's flagship Hackathon Challenge! This 24-hour coding marathon brings together the brightest minds to solve real-world problems through technology.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, this hackathon offers a platform to showcase your skills, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and create innovative solutions that can make a difference.

This year's theme is "Sustainable Technology Solutions" - focusing on how technology can address environmental challenges and promote sustainability.

What to Expect:

  • 24-hour coding challenge
  • Mentorship from industry experts
  • Networking opportunities with tech companies
  • Workshops and tech talks
  • Amazing prizes for winning teams

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to all students of the university
  • Teams of 2-4 members
  • At least one team member must have intermediate coding experience
  • All participants must bring their own laptops
Duty Leave Available: Full Day (May 15-16, 2023)

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have coding experience to participate?

While at least one team member should have intermediate coding experience, beginners are absolutely welcome! The hackathon is a learning experience, and we'll have mentors available to help you throughout the event.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

You should bring your laptop, charger, any necessary adapters, and your student ID. We'll provide food, drinks, and a comfortable workspace. You might also want to bring a change of clothes and toiletries for the overnight stay.

How are the projects judged?

Projects will be judged based on innovation, technical complexity, adherence to the theme, user experience, and presentation. The judging panel will consist of faculty members and industry professionals.

How do I get duty leave for the event?

Duty leave certificates will be provided to all registered participants. You can collect them at the registration desk on the day of the event or download them from your profile after the event.
